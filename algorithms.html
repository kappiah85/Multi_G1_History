Graphics algorithms (Canvas 2D)

Demonstrations of line rasterization (DDA), midpoint circle, quadratic Bézier curves, and Cohen–Sutherland clipping with window-to-viewport mapping. These complement the 3D pipeline (translation, rotation, scaling, perspective) handled by Three.js for the globe.
