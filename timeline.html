Continent timeline (algorithms)

Each region has its own vertical story. The spine is rasterized with DDA or Bresenham (your choice); events use the midpoint circle; smooth links are quadratic Bézier chords, each drawn as a line algorithm. Cohen–Sutherland clips segments to the inner window.
